FACULTY MANUAL Document File: Click here to view Faculty Manual
Overview of the CPSU Faculty Manual The Faculty Manual of Central Philippines State University – Moises Padilla Campus serves as a comprehensive guide for academic personnel, outlining the roles, responsibilities, and professional standards expected of faculty members. It covers key areas such as: Teaching and Instructional Duties Guidelines on lesson planning, classroom management, grading systems, and student engagement. Faculty Rights and Responsibilities Policies on academic freedom, ethical conduct, and professional development. Administrative Procedures Protocols for leave applications, performance evaluations, and institutional reporting. Research, Extension, and Community Involvement Encouragement of scholarly work and active participation in community-based initiatives. Code of Conduct and Disciplinary Measures Standards for behavior and procedures for addressing violations. This manual ensures that faculty members uphold CPSU’s mission of delivering quality education, fostering innovation, and promoting sustainable development across all campuses.
