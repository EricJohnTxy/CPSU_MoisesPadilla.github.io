Central Philippines State University Moises Padilla Campus History of Central Philippines State University - Moises Padilla Campus
Central Philippines State University – Moises Padilla Campus is a dynamic extension of CPSU committed to delivering quality, affordable, and accessible education to students in southern Negros Occidental. The campus offers various academic programs that emphasize practical skills, innovation, and sustainable development, aligned with the university’s mission to uplift lives through excellent instruction, research, and community engagement. Located in the heart of Moises Padilla, the campus provides a nurturing academic environment with a strong focus on agriculture, information technology, business, and education. CPSU Moises Padilla continues to grow as a center of learning, producing competent and responsible graduates ready to serve and lead in their communities.
Bachelor of Elementary Education (BEEd) Central Phillipines State University - Moises Padilla Campus The BEEd program at Central Philippines State University – Moises Padilla Campus is designed to prepare future educators with the knowledge, skills, and values needed to teach in elementary schools. The curriculum emphasizes child-centered teaching, curriculum development, classroom management, and inclusive education. Students gain hands-on experience through practice teaching and community engagement, shaping them into competent, compassionate, and innovative teachers ready to serve young learners across diverse settings.
Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) Central Philippines State University - Moises Padilla Campus The BSIT program at Central Philippines State University – Moises Padilla Campus equips students with practical and theoretical skills in software development, web technologies, networking, and database management. With a strong focus on hands-on learning, students build real-world projects and explore emerging tech trends. The program prepares future IT professionals to become innovative problem-solvers, ready to meet the demands of the digital world in both local and global industries.
Bachelor of Science in Agricultural Business (BSAB) Central Phillipines State University - Moises Padilla Campus The BSAB program at Central Philippines State University – Moises Padilla Campus blends agriculture and business to prepare students for careers in agribusiness management, farm entrepreneurship, and agricultural marketing. The curriculum covers topics such as agricultural economics, finance, supply chain, and agribusiness planning, equipping students with the skills to manage farms, agribusiness enterprises, and rural development projects. Graduates are empowered to become innovative leaders in the agricultural sector, driving sustainable growth in local and global markets.
Campus Overview
The general mandate of Central Philippines State University as indicated in Section 2 of the Republic Act No. 10228 states that "The University shall primarily provide advanced education, higher Technological professional instruction, and training in agriculture/fisheries, animal science, forestry, education, computer studies, engineering, arts and sciences and other relevant fields of study. It should also promote and undertake research, extension services and provide progressive leadership in its area of specialization". As stated in Section 3 of RA 10228 provides curricular offerings of the university, to wit: "The University shall offer undergraduate and graduate studies in the fields of agriculture/fisheries, animal science, forestry, education, computer studies, engineering, arts and sciences, short-term technical or diploma courses in agriculture technology and other degrees within in areas of specialization and accordance to its capabilities, as the Board of Regents may deem as necessary to carry out its objectives and to meet the needs of the Province of Negros Occidental and Region VI." Negros State College of Agriculture, Moises Padilla Campus face unto the majestic cone of Canlaon Volcano, surrounds by the verdant and beautiful nature. It is located in the locality of Moises Padilla, Negros Occidental with a distance of about 30 kms. away from the City of Canlaon and approximately 79 kms. away from the City of Bacolod. It is the pioneer State College established in the town of Moises Padilla through the effort of the local government headed by Mayor Francisco M. Nazareno and the Sangguniang Bayan Members at Brgy. 7, Moises Padilla, Negros Occidental of about 4.4 hectares of lot donated by the Hon. Mayor Francisco M. Nazareno and Family. Its purpose of establishing this Agricultural school is to hasten economic development of the country by training the youth in scientific agriculture and other allied courses. Board Resolution No. 39, s. 2007 officially created the Negros State College of Agriculture, Moises Padilla Campus on September 19, 2007. It became operation on June 10, 2008, with seven (7) classrooms building worth 3.0 million Provincial Government with comfort room through Gov. Isidro P. Zayco, Vice Gov. Emilio "Dino" Yulo III with seven hundred thousand pesos (P700,000.00) municipal counterpart through Mayor Francisco M. Nazareno, Vice mayor Cipriano P. Remo Jr. and the municipal council members together with the support of our very own President Freddie C. Maningo, Ph.D., CPA. NSCA, Moises Padilla Campus has been attracting students from different and cities of Negros Occidental and Negros Oriental. Its relevant and affordable curricular programs and its agricultural environment that is pleasant and conducive for learning, have constantly drawn clients who wish to acquire appropriate technology and skills for productive, decent and gainful living. NSCA is strongly committed to carrying out better Instruction, Production, Research, and Extension. All these functions have been carried out towards meeting the needs of students, out-of-school youth, and young adults, thus making them useful and productive citizens.
